Now, based on the circle theorm "Inscribed Angle Theorem" that states "The angle subtended by an arc at the centre of a circle is twice the angle subtended at any point on the circumfrence" this follows.

\[
2a = 2c \\
2b = 2c \\
\]

Therefore,

\[
2a = 2b \\
\boxed{a = b}
\]
